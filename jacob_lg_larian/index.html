In-Engine Narrative Design
Errant
narrative puzzle platformer - Showcased at IGDA booth for the 2024 SAAM Arcade
Errant is a puzzle platformer where you play as an amnesiac knight stuck in the realm of an enigmatic Fey. The Fey will barter for your lost memories in exchange for unlocking new sections of her twisting halls. However, each memory exchanged will also alter the letter that is your only clue to how you ended up here. Can you escape this labyrinth? What will you choose remember from before?

The Social Thread
social simulation game
Play as a college student with social anxiety trying to survive at a party. Talk to the other party goers. If you connect with them, your anxiety will stay under control. Otherwise, you will begin to spiral. Let your anxiety get too high, and you will be unable to stay at the party. But if you manage to tough it out until midnight, you might just get a chance to see Rowan again...
